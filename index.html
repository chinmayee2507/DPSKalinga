Academics

  • PRIMARY SCHOOL (NUR-2)

    At this level "interaction" is the key approach for the little ones. This facilitates the early development of a childâ€™s linguistic, numerical, motor, and kinesthetic & communication skills. The curriculum is "activity centred" where the teachers are loving, caring & implement an eclectic blend of Montessori an& traditional teaching approach. The classrooms are bright & wear a cheerful look bubbling with activity. The little ones also are taken to the splash pool for swimming under expert guidance; educational toys are used to ensure correct concept formation and a playground with slides, swings, seesaws, merry-go-rounds & a toy train. We have digital classroom teaching to make learning & teaching easy. The classrooms come alive with the use of 2D and 3D audio-visual animations that make even complicated concepts very easy to understand. Your child is with us: 7.30 am to 12.00pm.

  • JUNIOR SCHOOL (3-5)

    At this stage special emphasis is on communication & self expression. This sharpens their creativity & problem solving skills. At this stage too learning is "activity centered" whereby a variety of teaching aids (visual & audio) are used for learning to be at a cognitive level. They are taken to the Computer Lab to get first-hand experience. Your child is with us: 7:30 am to 1:40 pm.

  • MIDDLE/SECONDARY (6-8/9-10)

    At this level a gamut of activities are planned to make learning interesting. The students use the Math Lab, Psychology Lab, Language Lab, Social Lab, Computer Lab, Physics, Chemistry & Biology Labs, AV Hall etc to make learning effective & understanding of concepts Easy Projects & Assignments are also done.

  • SENIOR SECONDARY (11-12)

    DPS Kalinga offers its students all three streams of Science, Commerce and Humanities. Interest Aptitude and Competence are the key areas for selection of students into the streams. We meticulously plan the curriculum, so as to bring about optimum results. Empowering the children to face this tomorrow with success and that is what we aim to do. Our teachers are specialists in their fields and guide the students amicably.
